WFPB Tacos

Description

Vegan, potato-and-tofu filling, wfpb

Makes enough filling for 30ish small tortillas, i.e. leftovers for tomorrow with rice!

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs of potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 packages of firm tofu
  • nutritional yeast
  • paprika
  • black salt
  • tumeric
  • salt + pepper
  • toppings like tomatoes, avocado, and salsa.

Steps

  1. Add onion to a pot with some water and cook until soft, adding more water when needed
  2. After 5 minutes or so, add potatoes.
  3. Crumble tofu with hands into pot, keeping some larger chunks.
  4. Add paprika, black salt, tumeric, salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Cook another ten minutes until onions start to carmalize and then turn off the heat.
